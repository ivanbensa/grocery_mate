5 Recipe

Lemon Garlic Chicken

40 mins Easy 4 servings
Ingredients
  • 500 g Chicken Breast
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Lemon
  • Salt
Instructions
  1. Season chicken with salt and garlic.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Cook chicken until golden.
  4. Add lemon juice.
  5. Serve hot.
